Can't decide what to eat?

Take a look at nearby restaurants. Browse through 20 restaurants that are nearest to your current location and view their details. Directly navigate to your desired restaurant by just tapping the 'show in maps' button. Still can't decide? Use 'select random restaurant' to help you decide what to eat effortlessly!



Tap 'show in maps' button to navigate to your desired restaurants.

Use 'random restaurants' to let foodie pick a random restaurants for you.



What to cook?

Browse through the recipes. Ranging from American food to Japanese food, Pasta to Chicken cuisines, sure you can decide what to cook for your meal!



Search or browse through cuisines by categories or region.

Use 'show in youtube' to redirect to recipe's video on youtube.



A save place to save your desired restaurant or recipe.

Found your favourite restaurant or recipe and want to save it for future reference? Simply tap the bookmark icon in the details page to save a restaurant or recipe into 'saved items'.



Save a particular recipe/restaurant by tapping the bookmark icon.

Easily delete a saved item with just a tap.



(coming soon) Food delivery

Targeting small local businesses with their own runner, foodie's food delivery function provides a platform to enable restaurants and food stalls to post their food available for delivery. Users can browse through restaurants/food stalls to see what's available for delivery and place an order.
